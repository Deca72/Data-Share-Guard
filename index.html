Privacy Policy for Data Share Guard

Effective Date: [Sep 29 2024]

Introduction

At Data Share Guard, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension operates and provides clarity on what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. This policy applies solely to the use of the Data Share Guard extension.

Data Collection and Use

Data Share Guard does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. The extension operates locally within your browser to analyze web pages and detect potential privacy risks related to data collection and sharing practices. All operations happen on the client side, and no data is sent to external servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): We do not collect any information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable data.
  • Financial Information: No payment information or financial data is collected or processed by the extension.
  • Health Information: Data Share Guard does not collect or process any health-related information.
  • Authentication Information: The extension does not collect passwords, security questions, or any other login or authentication information.
  • Location Data: Data Share Guard does not track your geographical location or IP address.
  • Web History: The extension does not store or analyze your web browsing history. We only analyze the content of the currently active webpage, and no history of visited websites is logged or stored.

How Data Share Guard Works

Data Share Guard analyzes the contents of the webpage you are visiting in real time. It checks for specific privacy-related keywords and patterns to provide you with a risk assessment based on the site’s potential data collection and sharing practices. This process happens entirely within your browser, and no external servers or third parties are involved.

Permissions

In order to function properly, Data Share Guard requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: This allows the extension to analyze the content of the currently active webpage.
  • storage: This allows the extension to save user preferences locally within the browser. No data is sent to any external server.
  • scripting: The extension uses scripting to evaluate the content of web pages to assess privacy risks.

Third-Party Access and Sharing

Data Share Guard does not share any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect or store any data, there is no information available to share.

Security

While Data Share Guard does not collect or process any user data, we take precautions to ensure that our extension operates securely. The extension code is regularly reviewed and updated to prevent vulnerabilities. Additionally, the extension operates entirely within your browser and does not make outbound requests to external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective Date” at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how Data Share Guard protects your privacy.
